# App Store Notes

App name:
Rentory

Subtitle:
Private rental record organiser

Promotional text:
“Create a clear, private record of your rented home with rooms, photos, documents, timeline notes, local reports and backups.”

Description:
“Rentory helps tenants keep their rental records organised in one calm, private place.

Create a record for your rented home, add rooms, check condition details, attach photos, store useful documents and keep a simple timeline of key dates and notes. When you need everything together, create a clear report from the information you have added.

Export and import Rentory backups when you want to move or keep a copy of your records.

Rentory is private by design. No account is needed, and your records stay on your device by default.

Rentory does not give legal, financial or tenancy advice.”
