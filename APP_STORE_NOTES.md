# App Store Notes

App name:
Rentory

Subtitle:
Private rental records for tenants & landlords

Promotional text:
“Keep clear, private rental records — rooms, photos, tenancies, compliance reminders, documents and local PDF reports.”

Description:
“Rentory is a calm, private place to keep your rental records.

Pick a profile on first launch — Renter or Landlord — and Rentory tailors the prompts to how you actually use the app. You can switch between profiles at any time in Settings.

Renters get a defensible record of their rented home: rooms, condition checks, move-in and move-out photos, documents, timeline notes, deposit details and a local PDF report when they need everything together.

Landlords get the same record-keeping foundation plus tools for tracking tenancies (with multiple tenants), compliance dates (gas safety, EICR, EPC, periodic inspections, tenancy renewals), reminders and a landlord-shaped PDF that includes tenancies and reminders.

Export and import Rentory backups when you want to move records to a new device or keep a copy. Opt in to iCloud Sync if you want Rentory to keep your records in step across your own devices using your private iCloud — no account is needed.

Rentory is private by design. No sign-in. No analytics. No advertising. No tracking. Records stay on your device by default.

Rentory does not give legal, financial or tenancy advice.”

App Review notes:
“Rentory is a local-first rental record organiser for both tenants and landlords. The app does not provide legal, financial or tenancy advice. It does not contact landlords or tenants on the user's behalf, submit disputes, manage deposits or provide claims support. Any purchase uses Apple StoreKit. iCloud Sync is opt-in and uses the user's own private CloudKit database; Rentory does not operate any backend the user's data is sent to.

To explore both profile flows, open Settings → Sample data and load the sample set. The renter profile loads eight fictional records; switching to the landlord profile and loading samples adds six landlord records with tenancies, tenants and compliance reminders. Sample data is clearly marked and can be cleared at any time.”

Backups are created on your device. You choose where to save or share them.
