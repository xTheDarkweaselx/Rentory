# Backup And Sync

## Rentory without iCloud

Rentory works fully without iCloud. Your records stay on your device by default.

## iCloud

iCloud sync is optional. Rentory does not use a developer-owned backend.

In this version, Rentory can check whether iCloud is available on the device and explain what iCloud sync would do. Full live iCloud record sync is being held back until it can be enabled safely without risking local data.

## Backups

Backups are created locally on your device.

Backups can include:
- Rental records
- Rooms
- Checklist items
- Photos
- Documents
- Timeline events

Backups do not include:
- Temporary reports by default
- Purchase state
- App Lock state

You choose where to save or share backups.

## Import safety

Import validates a backup before changing local data.

If you choose to replace local data:
- Rentory validates the backup first
- It asks for confirmation
- It deletes local data only after that confirmation

## Privacy

- No Rentory backend is used
- No analytics are used
- No advertising or tracking are used
- Backups are not uploaded unless the user explicitly chooses an iCloud or Files location
